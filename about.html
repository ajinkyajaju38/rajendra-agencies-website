Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Quality

Rajendra Agencies was established with a single, clear vision — to make high-quality lab chemicals and scientific supplies easily accessible to the research, pharma, and educational community of Satara and surrounding Maharashtra.

Over the years, we have grown into a comprehensive one-stop destination for laboratories, hospitals, pharmaceutical manufacturers, and educational institutions. Our deep understanding of the scientific supply chain, combined with our commitment to quality, has earned us the trust of hundreds of clients across the region.

Today, we proudly stock products from India's leading brands including Merck, Borosil, SRL, Loba Chemie, HiMedia, and many more — all available under one roof at competitive prices.
